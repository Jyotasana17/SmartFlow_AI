# Smart City Traffic Rules & Regulations v1.2

## Section 1: Emergency Vehicle Priority
Emergency vehicles (ambulances, fire trucks, police) always have the highest priority in the SmartFlow AI system.
When an emergency vehicle is detected within 500 meters of a junction, the AI system must:
1. Identify the vehicle's trajectory.
2. Synchronize green signals along the emergency corridor.
3. Halt conflicting traffic flows to ensure clear passage.
4. Notify the command assistant of the activation.

## Section 2: Congestion Management
Congestion is defined as vehicle density exceeding 75% of lane capacity.
In cases of severe congestion (density > 90%), the AI controller should:
- Extend green phase timings by up to 20 seconds.
- Trigger AI rerouting suggestions for incoming traffic.
- Coordinate with adjacent junctions to balance load.

## Section 3: Accident Protocols
In the event of a detected accident:
- Immediately switch the affected junction to 'Safe Mode' (all-red or blinking amber).
- Alert emergency services via the notification module.
- The Command Assistant should provide the operator with the nearest available detour routes.
